About the Illustrator

Hello, my name is Shannon Damm. Born in a small town with not much to do, I found myself painting, drawing, illustrating and photographing anything and everything around me. I have had a passion for photography since I was 15 but there is not much of a future in a hobby so small. Being in a Dutch family, where couponing is a sport, I was told having lots of money doesn’t matter, but being told that so often, it kinda does matter. My mother always told me money won’t come from a man, so I guess that means I need a way to find it myself, applying my creative skills to something more. I began freelance illustrations in December of 2020 and my quarantine hobby became a small side hustle I still like to consider my favourite hobby.
